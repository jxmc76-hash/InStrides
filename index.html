Welcome to TrainingLog

Your personal fitness and wellbeing tracker. Here's everything you need to know.

📋

Daily Log

The main view of your activity history, organised by week. Each row is a day. Click any tick (✓) to edit an entry, or click the week label to expand or collapse that week.

➕

Log / Plan

Use the orange button to log a completed workout or plan a future one. Select a date, your mood, any custom metrics, the exercise type, and intensity. Planned entries show as ? in the log — click them to mark as done.

🏃

Exercise Types

Each exercise belongs to a category: Distance (e.g. Run, Swim), Weight (e.g. Gym), Reps (e.g. bodyweight), or Other. When logging, the relevant metric input appears automatically — distance in km, weight in kg, reps, or a 1–10 effort score.

⚙️

Types

Manage your exercise types — add, rename, or delete them, and set their category. Your types appear as columns in the Daily Log.

📊

Custom Metrics

Track anything beyond mood — sleep quality, nutrition, stress. Add a metric as a Yes/No toggle or a 1–10 slider. These appear as extra columns in the log and feed into the Insights page. If you add one named SLEEP, it will appear automatically in the 10-day comparison.

💡

Insights

Automatically generated from your data. See how your last 10 days compare to the prior 10, track distance covered over time, and watch your mood trend. The more you log, the more useful this becomes.

🟠

Strava

Connect your Strava account once using the Connect Strava button. After that, click ✓ Strava to pull in your latest activity and log it with one tap — date, distance and activity name are pre-filled for you.
